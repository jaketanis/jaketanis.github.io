EDEN Tour Poster

Situation

I am a huge fan of EDEN. I love the aesthetic he communicates through his music. So, I wanted to apply that aesthetic to one of his tour flyer for 2020.

Task & Action

The main task involved with this project was to make the visuals more attuned to his “no future” album, which is an edit of the photograph used for the tour schedule. Off-white/pale yellow was the color scheme that seemed to match with the photographs aesthetic. After that, it was about arranging what was already on the original tour poster onto this new one. I decided to make all of the text lower-case (except for the artist’s names). This is how each of his songs are titled on the album. I also wanted to reference the small red dot shown in the photo, so I edited the last few words of the poem to be red. I also added the red square in the top right corner of the flyer.

Results

After completing this project, I posted it onto a reddit fan page. It seemed to be well received among the fanbase.
