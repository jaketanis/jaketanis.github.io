Usability Testing for Quora

Situation

In one of my graduate classes at the University of North Texas, we conducted our own research to see how implementing plain language to a piece of writing could improve the usability of that writing. I chose Quora’s “About Us” statement to implement plan language practices to.

Task and Action

My research was conducted using A & B testing methods. I presented a survey to my classmates that contained Quora’s original “About Us” message along with some questions that pertained to the usability of that message. To another group of classmates, I presented a survey that contained a plain language version of Quora’s “About Us” statement as well as accompanying questions. The goal of the surveys was to gain data on whether implementing plain language practices to Quora’s introductory statement would improve its usability for readers.

Result

After analyzing the survey’s answers, the results showed that the plain language version of Quora’s “About Us” message improved the usability of the message. The following images display the data supporting the usability of the altered message over the original message. This project helped me to practice my plain language writing techniques, as well as get some experience with usability research. It was very enjoyable and interesting to see data supporting plain language usage.
